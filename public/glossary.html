Glossary

  • Bayou: Slow-moving, marshy waterway that helps manage floodwaters (e.g., Buffalo Bayou).
  • Recycled water: Treated wastewater used for drinking, irrigation, and industry.
  • Resilience: Ability to recover from natural and human-made challenges.
  • Subsidence: Sinking of land due to groundwater extraction.
  • Sustainability: Meeting present needs without compromising future generations.
  • Water Equity: Fair inclusion in access, resilience, and water decision-making.
