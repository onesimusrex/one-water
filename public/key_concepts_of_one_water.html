Key Concepts of One Water

Resilience & Sustainability
  • Promoting economic, social, and environmental sustainability
  • Balancing quantity and quality with rising demand
  • Managing flooding and extreme weather
Interconnected Water Systems
  • Understanding that our water and our interactions with it are all connected
  • Evaluating infrastructure improvements that can drive change
  • Accounting for the nexus between water, energy, the built environment, and food security
Water Equity
  • Planning on the basis that affordability and access to water is critical
  • Fostering community-driven decision making to help ensure policies do not unfairly exclude certain populations
  • Implementing projects with equitable benefits and impacts
